Keyboard

The idea for this is to fully design my own keyboard, pcb and all.

I know that I want to have the case made out of acrylic, as it is an easy and cheap material to work with, and I like the design.

The biggest hurdle to get over will definitely be learning the PCB design.

Here are the parts each ranked by difficulty.

  1. PCB Design
  2. Case Design
  3. PCB Soldering
  4. Switch Soldering
  5. All the other parts of assembly (keycaps, stabilizers, etc.)
